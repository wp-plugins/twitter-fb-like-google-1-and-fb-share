A very simple social share plugin with just 4 social icons (Twitter, FB Like, Google +1 and FB Share) 

== Description ==
A very simple social share plugin with just 4 social icons. Well the around 99% of the posts are share with these 4 social icons (i.e Twitter, FB Like, Google +1 and FB Share) then why to have more. 

More info:  http://emsphere.info/wp-plugin/twitter-fb-like-google-1-and-fb-share.html 


== Installation ==

1. Upload `ftgshare` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Goto the Settings > Facebook , twitter , google to change setting
